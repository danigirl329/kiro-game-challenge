# Product Overview

**Super Kiro World** is a browser-based 2D platformer game featuring the Kiro mascot as the playable character.

## Core Gameplay
- Side-scrolling platformer with physics-based movement
- Collect coins (10 points) and gems (25-50 points) across the level
- Navigate platforms of varying heights to reach the goal
- Lives system with respawn mechanics
- Score tracking and win/lose conditions

## Player Experience
- Responsive controls using Arrow Keys or WASD
- Smooth camera follow system
- Visual feedback through Kiro brand colors
- Instant restart capability (press R)
- Clear UI showing score and remaining lives

## Target Audience
Workshop participants at AWS Re:Invent learning game development with vanilla JavaScript.
